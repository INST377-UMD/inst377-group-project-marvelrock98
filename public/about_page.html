About the J&J Law Firm App


Currently, many law firms rely on manual data entry and record-keeping, which can be time-consuming, error-prone, and can ultimately lead to extreme inefficiency. We aim to solve this issue by streamlining the process of case information collection and the management of a law firm. No longer will lawyers or those within a law firm have to manually search the web to find information on a specific case. Instead, they will now be able to simply enter the name of the case into our application, and they will instantly be able to see a description of the case as well as what state the case took place in. Additionally, lawyers will be able to enter new cases that they are not already able to find on application, that way the database of court cases will be ever growing to make life easier for all those out there who are concerned with court cases.
